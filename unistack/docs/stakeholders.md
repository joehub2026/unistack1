# Stakeholders — UniStack

## Primary Stakeholders

### 1. Students (End Users)
- **Who**: All enrolled students at INES-Ruhengeri
- **Goal**: Post items for sale, find housing, read announcements, flag suspicious posts
- **Pain point**: Current WhatsApp system is unorganized and unsafe
- **Access level**: Can post, browse, flag; cannot publish without approval

### 2. Moderators (Content Reviewers)
- **Who**: Appointed student reps or staff with content review duties
- **Goal**: Review and approve/reject student submissions
- **Pain point**: No current structured way to review or moderate community posts
- **Access level**: Can approve/reject/flag posts; no user management

### 3. Admin (Platform Manager)
- **Who**: IT department or designated INES staff
- **Goal**: Manage all users, oversee the platform, maintain integrity
- **Pain point**: No oversight tool exists today
- **Access level**: Full access — user management, all content, system logs

## Secondary Stakeholders
- **INES Management**: Benefits from reduced scam reports and a safer campus digital environment
- **Future Employers / Alumni**: Could benefit from an extended version with job postings
- **Local Landlords**: May benefit if the housing board is extended publicly (future scope)
